About Tsawq.ae

UAE's fastest-growing online supermarket, delivering fresh groceries to your doorstep in 60 minutes.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Tsawq.ae was born from a simple idea: everyone in the UAE deserves access to fresh, quality groceries without the hassle of traffic and long checkout lines.

Starting with just 500 products in Dubai Marina, we've grown to serve over 50,000 customers across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah with 10,000+ products.

Our name "Tsawq" means "market" in Arabic - and we're proud to be the modern digital market for the UAE community.
